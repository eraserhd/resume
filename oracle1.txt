The OCI Physical Networking team is building a new Infrastructure-as-Code orchestration platform to test and deploy configuration and firmware updates across our worldwide fleet. In this role, you will contribute to the development of this new orchestration platform in partnership with network engineers. 

You should be passionate and highly skilled in programming in at least one programming language including C++/Go/Java/Python. Writing structured and optimized code for improving performance and scalability when it is deployed in a cloud environment as a part of a service operating in a distributed environment is one of the most critical requirements.
Responsibilities

As a member of the team developing a new orchestration and test platform, you will work independently and deliver projects by:

        Leading the development and delivery of major features end-to-end
        Participating in all phases of the SDLC including requirements refinement, design, development, testing, release and post release service operations
        Providing mentorship to junior engineers 
        Writing correct, secure, maintainable and robust code with appropriate tests. 
        Improving the team engineering practices, ops practices, development process, ownership, mentoring. 
        Influencing partner teams
        Leading and Driving the design and architecture of your product/service along with operational readiness & excellence for the same
